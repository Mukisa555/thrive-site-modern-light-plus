Meet Our Team

Our leadership team guides Thrive Path with strategic clarity, educational depth, and long-term commitment to learner transformation across Uganda and beyond.

Managing Director (MD)

Founder and Managing Director

Provides overall strategic leadership, institutional direction, and day-to-day management to ensure high-quality program delivery and long-term growth.

Chief Program Officer

Program Leadership

Owns program architecture and delivery quality, ensuring every learning track remains practical, relevant, and learner-centered across partner schools.

Head of Learning & Innovation

Learning and Innovation

Leads continuous content improvement and innovation cycles so learners build future-ready competence aligned to real opportunities and local realities.

Partnerships & Impact Director

Partnerships and Impact

Builds and stewards collaborations with schools, institutions, and partners while translating implementation into measurable outcomes and impact reporting.

Monitoring & Evaluation Lead

Impact Measurement

Designs monitoring frameworks, tracks learner and school outcomes, and supports evidence-based reporting for partners, schools, and program improvement cycles.

School Partnerships Coordinator

School Engagement

Coordinates onboarding and support for partner schools, aligns implementation plans with school realities, and ensures smooth communication across stakeholders.

Training & Capacity Lead

Facilitator Development

Leads training programs for teachers, counsellors, and facilitators, including onboarding, refresher modules, and practical delivery coaching.

Digital Learning & Analytics Lead

Data and Learning Tools

Oversees digital tools and analytics dashboards that support delivery visibility, learner engagement tracking, and real-time decision-making.

Curriculum Development Lead

Content Quality

Maintains and updates Thrive Path modules to keep content practical, locally relevant, and aligned with future-ready skills and opportunities.

Innovation & AI Labs Coordinator

Innovation Programs

Coordinates innovation challenges and AI learning activities, helping schools implement safe, practical, and high-impact technology experiences.

Leadership approach

How the team operates together

  • Strategize: Leadership aligns mission, annual priorities, and scale targets.
  • Implement: Program and school teams execute delivery with clear standards.
  • Evaluate: Learning outcomes, implementation feedback, and partner reports are reviewed.
  • Strengthen: Decisions are refined for stronger impact and long-term resilience.

Grounded in the Thrive Path concept papers

This team model reflects the direction in your school, policy, and investor documents: structured leadership, practical implementation, measurable outcomes, and strong partnership stewardship.
